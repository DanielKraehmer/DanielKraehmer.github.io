Stata

I am maintaining a growing collection of tips and resources for coding in Stata. The materials cover anything from workflow issues to useful (but seemingly unknown) Stata functions to handy graphing commands and recommendations how to make one's do-files reproducible. The "Clever Coding" series was initiated as part of a learning hour at LMU Munich's Department of Sociology and is updated continuously.
